How to Play

  1. Choose your language: Filipino or English.
  2. Select your word length: 5, 6, or 7 letters.
  3. Enter your guess and see which letters are correct or in the wrong place.
  4. Use the feedback to narrow down the correct word.
  5. Switch themes and check “My Achievements” as you progress.
  6. (Optional) Tap “Sync progress via Google Play” to save your progress and load it on another device.
